Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2/21/2025

Jane Music LLC ("Company", "we", "us") values your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how it is protected when you use the Şarkı Evreni app ("App").

1. Data We Collect

Our app may collect the following types of data:

  • Device Information: Your device model, operating system version and network information.
  • Usage Data: In-app interactions, downloaded content, and usage durations.
  • Crash Reports: Technical information automatically collected in case of app crashes or errors.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

The data we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide the core functionality of the app.
  • To improve and personalize the user experience.
  • To monitor app performance and resolve errors.
  • To optimize ad revenue through limited data sharing with ad networks.

3. Data Sharing

Collected data is shared with third parties only in the following cases:

  • Ad Networks: Ad networks like AppLovin MAX use limited data to optimize ad delivery.
  • Analytics and Monitoring: Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics are used to process usage statistics and crash reports.
  • Legal Requirements: Data may be shared to comply with legal regulations or court orders.

4. Data Security

Collected data is stored in an encrypted format and is accessible only to authorized personnel. We implement industry-standard measures to ensure data security.

5. Your Rights

As a user, you have the following rights:

  • To request access to your collected data.
  • To request the deletion or correction of your data.
  • To object to data collection practices.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at janemusicapps@proton.me.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not designed for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them. If you believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

7. Policy Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be announced within the app or on our website.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us at janemusicapps@proton.me.
