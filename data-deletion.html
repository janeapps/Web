Data Deletion

Thank you for choosing Şarkı Evreni. Privacy and the security of user data are of utmost importance to us. Therefore, we have created a dedicated page to make it easy for our users to request the deletion of their data.

Steps for Data Deletion Request:

  1. Log in to the Google Play store: Şarkı Evreni (Google Play Store)
  2. Select the application listed under "Şarkı Evreni" on the store entry screen.
  3. Inside the application, click on the three-dot icon located in the upper right corner.
  4. Choose the option "Clear Search History" from the menu that appears.
  5. By following these steps, you can request the deletion of your search history stored on your device.

Data Types and Retention Periods:

The Şarkı Evreni application only records your search history on your device. This data is permanently deleted the moment you, as the user, choose to delete it. There is no additional retention period.

Note:

  • We respect the privacy of our users and ensure the highest level of data security.
  • Our application does not have user membership, ensuring the safety of your personal information.
  • Data is immediately deleted when you uninstall the application or submit a deletion request.

Thank you once again for choosing Şarkı Evreni. If you have any questions, feel free to reach out to our Support Team.
